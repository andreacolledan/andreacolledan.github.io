# Projects

## QuRA
[View on github](https://github.com/andreacolledan/qura)

QuRA is a tool for resource-aware quantum programming. It analyzes programs written in the PQ language, which lets programmers describe quantum computations with type-based resource bounds, such as maximum qubit usage or circuit depth. QuRA automatically verifies these bounds and compiles PQ programs into quantum circuits that are ready for execution on real quantum hardware or simulators.


QuRA is the product of [my doctoral thesis](https://amsdottorato.unibo.it/id/eprint/12082/).
